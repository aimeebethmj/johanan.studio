Deep down I've always been a nature girl. One of those tree-hugging, sing-to-the-flowers children.

But for many years I forgot who I was,
Taking a detour in fashion, wearable-tech and web design.
Miserable and lost for most of it...

Until I started to question:
Who am I really?
Who is it I'm showing to the world?
I couldn't see that sing-to-the-flowers girl anymore

Now every
day that passes,
I'm uncovering
her again

Returning to art
to uplift myself &
everyone around me

If you can relate to this,
maybe it could help you too

In our commuter-grey day-to-day,
can you find a safe space to express your true nature?

I think
that's where
colour & pattern
come in...

I'm on a mission to uplift.
And to remind you that you can be vivid, vibrant and full of positive energy

I hope to inspire you to surround yourself with swathes of colour...
Let's draw out the real you.
